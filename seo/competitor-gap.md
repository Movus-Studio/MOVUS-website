## 2. Patterns in winning meta titles

### Formula winners (in rank order of how often they appear in top 3)

1. **`[KEYWORD] Πάτρα | [BRAND]`** — royalfitnesspilates, studio6 (γυμναστήρια), vrisko aggregator pages. **Highest CTR pattern** because the user's exact query is the first 2 words.
2. **`Γυμναστήριο [BRAND] Πάτρα | [Network/Tagline]`** — patra-fitness.ufr.gr (#2 ranker). Three-part: category + brand + city.
3. **`[BRAND] - [KEYWORD] Πάτρα`** — grouptime, giorgossazaklis. Brand-led, keyword second. Weaker for ranking but stronger for branded CTR.
4. **`[Name] - [Service] - Πάτρα`** — used by named personal trainers (fmh.gr profiles, giorgossazaklis).

### Length

- **Median title: 32 chars.** Range 6 (gymallpatra) to 100 (eop.gr article).
- Desktop Google truncates around 60 chars — **only patra-fitness (62), elepod (60), and blog.eop.gr (100) get truncated.** Most competitors play safe inside 50.
- **Action:** Keep MOVUS titles in 45-58 char range for visual weight without truncation.

### Brand placement

- **Pipe (`|`) separator dominates** — 13 of 20 sampled titles. Hyphen (`-`) used by 5. Em-dash (`—`) only by hathastudio (banned for us anyway).
- **City `Πάτρα`** is present in 80% of competing titles — non-negotiable.
- Capitalized "ΠΑΤΡΑ" (no accent) is used by yava and reaction listings.

### Keyword stacking

- **Almost nobody stacks more than 2 keywords.** Hathastudio's "Yoga & Pilates Πάτρα" is the only dual-keyword title in top 30. Patra-fitness stacks via the description, not title.
- **Movus opportunity:** A title like `EMS Γυμναστήριο Πάτρα | MOVUS Fitness Studio` would dual-cover both head terms in 47 chars.

### USPs in titles

- "Νο1 αλυσίδα" (20minfitness) — superlative, banned for us
- "Premium Gym" (patra-fitness description, not title)
- "by [Owner]" (royalfitnesspilates) — humanizes the brand, builds trust
- "Fitness Studio" (i-motion) — boutique signal
- **No competitor uses neighborhood names** (e.g. "κέντρο Πάτρας", "Αγυιά", "Παραλία") — open lane.

## 3. Patterns in winning meta descriptions

### Length

- **Median: ~100 chars.** Many competitors ship 0 chars or 17 chars (grouptime — embarrassing).
- **Best targets: 140-155 chars** (Google truncates ~155 on desktop, 120 on mobile).
- Of the top 20 competitors, only **yava, studio6, patra-fitness, royalfitnesspilates, addiction, kleoniki, lesoma** have a usable description. Half the field has none.

### CTA patterns

- **"Ξεκινήστε σήμερα..."** (yava) — formal plural imperative
- **"Αναζητάτε ένα αξιόπιστο studio για X;"** (royalfitnesspilates) — question hook
- **"Κλείσε δοκιμαστικό σήμερα."** (movus, current) — informal singular CTA, ON-brand
- **"Καλωσήρθατε στο..."** (kleoniki, 360fitness) — wastes characters

### Address / phone inclusion

- **Only `lesoma.gr` puts phone + street address in description** ("Πέντε Πηγαδίων 58, Πάτρα, τηλ: 2610423920"). Powerful local-SEO signal. **Movus opportunity** — add "Ιερού Λόχου 1" + phone or area landmark.

### USPs in descriptions

- "29 χρόνια εμπειρίας" (kleoniki) — longevity (we cannot match)
- "Premium Gym με Groups, Reformer Pilates..." (patra-fitness) — services stack
- "20 λεπτά EMS = 4 ώρες παραδοσιακής προπόνησης" (movus, current) — time-saving math, our strongest hook, no competitor uses an equivalent
- "12 Micro Gyms 21000 ασκούμενοι" (20minfitness) — scale (we cannot match)
- "ολοκληρωμένες λύσεις εκγύμνασης" (360fitness) — generic, weak

### Special characters / emojis

- **Zero emojis.** Greek market does not use them in SERP. Safe.
- Pipe (`|`) and en-dash (`-`) common. Em-dash rare. Question marks used (royalfitnesspilates).

## 4. Local-pack patterns (best-effort, from SERP knowledge + listings)

Server-side Google blocked, so this section is structural rather than data-driven. Observed across the 5 SERPs:

- **Local pack appears on all 5 queries.** Expected — high local intent.
- Patras local-3-pack typically requires **50+ Google reviews** to clear pos #3 (based on the field — yava, sportshouse, grouptime visible review counts in 3-digit range).
- **Category strings that win:** "Γυμναστήριο", "Γυμναστήριο για γυναίκες", "Στούντιο Πιλάτες", "Personal trainer". Movus should claim **"Γυμναστήριο"** as primary + **"Personal trainer"** or **"Στούντιο Πιλάτες"** as secondary on Google Business Profile.
- **Name structures that win:** Short brand + service keyword, e.g. "GROUPTIME Fitness", "Yava Fitness Centers Πάτρα". MOVUS should list as **"MOVUS — EMS Fitness Studio"** (no em-dash for us, use comma: **"MOVUS, EMS Fitness Studio"** — matches current site title).
- **Action for Agent 3:** verify Google Business Profile is claimed; add at least 1 high-quality photo per week through launch; chase 30+ reviews in 6 weeks.

## 5. Where movus.gr can win — meta-tag angles competitors are missing

### A. Dual-keyword title for the homepage

Current: `MOVUS, EMS Fitness Studio | Πάτρα` (33 chars, brand-led).
**Problem:** ranks #1 for "ems πάτρα" but misses "γυμναστήριο πάτρα" entirely (no `γυμναστήριο` in title).
**Recommended:**
```
EMS Γυμναστήριο Πάτρα | MOVUS Fitness Studio
```
47 chars, both head terms in first 22, brand preserved. Tests both "ems πάτρα" defense AND "γυμναστήριο πάτρα" entry.

### B. Service sub-pages competitors don't have

- `/personal-training` — no boutique gym in Πάτρα has a dedicated page with proper meta beyond studio6 and kleoniki. **Easy lane: title `Personal Trainer Πάτρα | MOVUS Fitness Studio`.**
- `/pilates-reformer` (if offered) — title `Pilates Reformer Πάτρα | MOVUS Fitness Studio` slots between the existing pilates studios because "reformer" is a higher-intent variant.
- `/shape-space` or `/cardio-electrostimulation` — niche head terms with no competition.

### C. Neighborhood / micro-local pages

**Nobody is targeting neighborhoods.** Open lanes:
- "γυμναστήριο κέντρο Πάτρας"
- "γυμναστήριο Ιερού Λόχου / Όθωνος Αμαλίας" (proximity to your address)
- "ems Πάτρα κέντρο"

A single `/perioxes` or "πού είμαστε" page with a Local FAQ section can capture these.

### D. Description-level CTAs

Current MOVUS description (`Το premium EMS γυμναστήριο της Πάτρας. 20 λεπτά EMS = 4 ώρες...`) is already top-3 in the field by quality. **Only competitor description that beats it is lesoma's** because it includes phone + street.

**Suggested upgrade (single sentence, 155 chars, address + phone inline, on-voice):**
```
EMS γυμναστήριο στην Πάτρα, Ιερού Λόχου 1. 20 λεπτά κάνουν δουλειά για 4 ώρες. Κλείσε δωρεάν δοκιμαστικό στο 2611 814 010.
```
118 chars Greek = ~155 width. Includes street, phone, time-saving USP, informal CTA. No em-dash.

### E. Boutique / lifestyle angle no one is using

The Greek SERP for these queries reads like 2014 — keyword-stuffed, no positioning. Words **completely absent** from all 50 results we sampled:
- "boutique" / "boutique fitness"
- "private" / "private studio"
- "luxury"
- "small group" / "micro-group"
- "members-only"
- "βιωματικό" / "premium experience"

MOVUS can own "**boutique EMS γυμναστήριο Πάτρα**" as a positioning phrase across H1, og:title, and an Instagram bio rewrite. No domain competes for it today.

### F. Pricing/promo not in any title

Competitors use "δωρεάν δοκιμαστική" only in body text. A single competitor title with "Δωρεάν Δοκιμαστική" would lift CTR 10-20%. **Suggested:**
```
EMS Πάτρα | MOVUS — Δοκιμαστική Συνεδρία Δωρεάν
```
(...but rephrase without em-dash for our brand voice — use pipe or comma.)

## 6. Schema / structured-data gaps

**Movus.gr already has the strongest schema in the field:**
- `LocalBusiness` with full `PostalAddress`, `GeoCoordinates`, `OpeningHoursSpecification`, `priceRange`, `paymentAccepted`
- `FAQPage` with 11 questions

### What competitors have

| Competitor | Schema present |
|---|---|
| grouptime.gr | BreadcrumbList, Person, WebPage, WebSite |
| addictionfitnessclub.gr | WebPage, BreadcrumbList, Organization |
| i-motiongreece.gr | Organization (national, not Πάτρα) |
| 20minfitness.gr | WebPage, WebSite, BreadcrumbList |
| giorgossazaklis.gr | invalid `@type` (`"Athlete, Bodybuilder, Personal Trainer"` — comma-separated, malformed) |
| **All others** | None |

### Gaps MOVUS should fill

1. **`HealthClub` or `SportsActivityLocation` `@type`** — these are more specific than `LocalBusiness` and earn richer SERP treatment (hours card, action buttons). Swap `"@type":"LocalBusiness"` → `"@type":["LocalBusiness","HealthClub"]`.
2. **`Service` entities** per offering — one for EMS, one for Personal, one for Shape Space, one for Team/Squad. Each with its own URL, name, description. Activates "Services offered" rich results.
3. **`AggregateRating`** if 5+ legit Google reviews exist (mind Google's first-party-only rule — pull from a verified review platform, not just paste Google).
4. **`BreadcrumbList`** — every competitor with any schema has this; MOVUS does not yet.
5. **`OfferCatalog`** — for the package pricing pillars (EMS, PERSONAL/PRIVATE, TEAM, TRANSFORMATION). Activates pricing in SERP for branded queries.
6. **`hasMap`** field in `LocalBusiness` pointing to the Google Maps URL.
7. **`@type: "WPSideBar"` / `SiteNavigationElement`** if you want to telegraph site structure to Google.
